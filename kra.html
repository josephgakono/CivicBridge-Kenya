KRA & Tax Basics

A simple guide to KRA PIN, iTax basics, filing habits, and avoiding common penalties. Plain language, no “legal spellcasting.”

KRA PIN: What you need

Quick note

A KRA PIN is often required for jobs, bank services, registration processes, and some government-related services.

  • ✓

Filing: simple workflow

  1. Confirm your filing category

    Employed, self-employed, student, etc. File the category that matches your situation.

  2. Prepare your information

    Income details and any relevant documents you rely on for accuracy.

  3. Log in and fill carefully

    Don’t rush. One wrong number can cause headaches later.

  4. Submit and keep proof

    Save confirmation details or evidence of filing.

Common mistakes

Avoid these

Wrong PIN details, mixing categories, skipping proof/confirmation, and “I’ll do it later” syndrome.

FAQ

Do I need to file if I have no income?

Many people still file to stay compliant depending on their category. Always confirm what applies to your situation.

What if I used the wrong details?

Correcting details can take time. It’s better to confirm accuracy before submitting.
