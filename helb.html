HELB & Student Finance

A guide to preparing for an application, tracking expectations, and organizing your student finances.

What you should prepare

Tip

Have your documents and details ready before you start. Most errors happen because people rush.

  • ✓
    Personal details. Accurate names and ID info matching official documents.
  • ✓
    School information. Your institution and admission details.
  • ✓
    Contacts. Active email and phone contacts you can access.
  • ✓
    Supporting documents. Any requested confirmations or proofs.

Simple workflow

  1. Confirm eligibility

    Make sure your situation matches the application category.

  2. Fill details carefully

    Names, numbers, and dates should be accurate.

  3. Submit and save proof

    Keep any confirmation page or reference number.

  4. Track your status

    Check updates and respond to requirements early.

FAQ

What if I make a mistake in my details?

Fixing errors can take time. Double-check before submitting to avoid delays.

How do I manage the money when it comes?

Budget essentials first: fees, rent, food, transport. Avoid impulse spending.
