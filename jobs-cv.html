Jobs & CV Toolkit

How to build a clean CV, prepare for interviews, and avoid common job traps.

CV structure (simple and effective)

Header

Name, phone, email, location, LinkedIn (optional).

Profile summary

3–4 lines: what you do + your strengths.

Skills

List relevant skills only. Keep it short.

Experience / Projects

Use bullet points and measurable impact.

Interview checklist

Know the company, practice common questions, and prepare 2–3 stories about your work.

FAQ

Should I include a photo on my CV?

Usually optional. Focus more on clarity and skills unless specifically requested.

How long should my CV be?

1 page if you’re starting out, 2 pages if you have strong experience.
