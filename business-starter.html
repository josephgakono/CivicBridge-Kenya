Business Starter Pack

A simple guide to starting small, keeping records, and avoiding common beginner mistakes.

Start with basics

Reality check

Most businesses fail because of poor records and weak customer handling, not because of “bad luck”.

Simple steps

  1. Pick a clear product/service

    Know what you sell and who needs it.

  2. Track money daily

    Record sales, expenses, and profit. Even on paper.

  3. Separate personal and business cash

    Mixing money destroys clarity.

  4. Build trust

    Deliver consistently and communicate clearly.

FAQ

Do I need a big budget to start?

No. Start small, prove demand, then scale carefully.

How do I price correctly?

Calculate cost + profit, then compare to competitors. Don’t guess blindly.
