IDs & Passport Guide

Requirements, step-by-step process, and common mistakes. Simple and readable, not “government PDF energy.”

Requirements Checklist

Tip

Bring originals + clear photocopies. Most delays are missing documents, not bad luck.

  • ✓
    Proof of identity. National ID / Birth certificate (depending on service).
  • ✓
    Passport photos. Recent, clear, correct background where required.
  • ✓
    Supporting documents. School letter / guardian details / affidavits if applicable.
  • ✓
    Payment proof. Receipt or reference number for fees (where required).

Step-by-step Process

  1. Choose the service type

    New application, replacement, correction, or passport application.

  2. Prepare documents

    Bring originals + copies. Ensure names match across documents.

  3. Submit application

    Fill details carefully and double-check spellings and ID numbers.

  4. Biometrics / verification

    Follow the process for fingerprints or photo capture where needed.

  5. Track and collect

    Keep receipt/tracking info safe for collection.

FAQ

What if names don’t match across documents?

You may need supporting documents (like an affidavit) before approval.

Can I apply without photocopies?

Sometimes, but it often delays processing. Copies help avoid repeat trips.
